Continue improving Nuelz Forex AI Assistant.

The current AI is too conservative and rarely produces trading opportunities. Redesign the analysis engine to provide more useful market insights while maintaining high-quality signal selection.

## Smarter AI Decision System

Do not rely solely on RSI, EMA, and MACD.

Use a combination of:

- RSI (7)
- EMA 50
- EMA 200
- MACD
- Price Action
- Support and Resistance
- Trend Strength
- Volatility
- Candlestick Pattern Recognition
- Multi-Timeframe Analysis

The AI should combine all available evidence before making a recommendation.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Multi-Level Confidence System

Instead of only producing signals above one threshold, classify every opportunity into one of three categories.

### 🟢 High Confidence (85–100%)

This is the strongest category.

Display:

🔥 HIGH CONFIDENCE TRADE

These setups have the highest level of technical confirmation.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

### 🟡 Medium Confidence (70–84%)

Display:

⚡ GOOD TRADING OPPORTUNITY

Explain which indicators support the trade and which confirmations are still missing.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

### 🔵 Watchlist (60–69%)

Display:

👀 WATCHLIST

This is not yet a recommended trade.

Explain why the market is interesting and what conditions would increase confidence.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Continuous Scanning

The AI must continuously scan the market every 30–60 seconds.

Keep updating internal analysis in the background.

Do not send automatic trading signals unless enabled by the owner.

When the user presses /signal, immediately return the latest and strongest available opportunities.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Better Signal Selection

If multiple currency pairs qualify, rank them from strongest to weakest.

Return only the number of signals requested by the user.

Never generate low-quality signals just to fill the requested number.

If the user requests three signals but only one qualifies, return that one signal and explain that no additional setups currently meet the confidence requirements.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Signal Explanation

Every recommendation must explain WHY it was selected.

Example:

📊 AI Analysis

✅ RSI indicates oversold momentum.

✅ Price is above both EMA 50 and EMA 200.

✅ MACD shows bullish momentum.

✅ Market trend is strong.

✅ Volatility supports potential continuation.

💡 AI Summary:

This setup has multiple technical confirmations across different indicators, making it one of the strongest opportunities currently available. This is an analysis, not a guarantee of market direction.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Market Status

If no High Confidence trades exist, do not simply say "No signal."

Instead, show the best current market overview.

Example:

📊 Market Overview

EUR/USD — 82% (Good Opportunity)

GBP/USD — 68% (Watchlist)

AUD/USD — 55% (No Setup)

USD/CAD — 74% (Good Opportunity)

This gives the user useful information even when there is no top-tier trade.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Learning and Performance Tracking

Store every generated signal and its outcome (when outcome data becomes available).

Track:

- Date
- Asset
- Direction
- Confidence Score
- Indicators Used
- Suggested Duration
- Result (if available)

Use this data to generate performance reports and improve future versions of the AI.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

## Reliability

The assistant must never promise guaranteed profits or guaranteed winning trades.

Its purpose is to identify the strongest available opportunities, explain the reasoning behind each recommendation, and help the user make informed trading decisions.

Focus on consistency, transparency, and professional-quality market analysis rather than generating frequent signals.
